spoapo - concept voice assistant for students.

Problem

  • Students haven't interest and motivation to study;
  • Parents spend a lot of time to check child's homework;
  • Students haven't partner to learn foreign English.

Solution

A voice assistant that helps a child learn. It can be implemented as a skill for Siri.

Opportunities:

Checking homework, without parents control.

  • The student can tell the assistant a poem, and the assistant will tell where child made a mistake;
  • Can check if the child remembered the dates correctly from history;
  • Voice akinator. "If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough." (Albert Einstein). It works like this: student explain assistant any topics, then assistant analyze said and guess what talking student about. If understands, then student correctly explained.

Learning a foreign language.

The assistant says words on English language and ask student repeat. Student repeats words, assistant analyze, then transform in text and compare with words, that he has as correct answer. If the words match, the assistant praises the child. If not, then kindly asks to try again. Simple dialogs can also work. Child will be learn understand foreign speech.

Inclusive education.

The assistant will help visually impaired children and children with cerebral palsy to get more information about world and study.
